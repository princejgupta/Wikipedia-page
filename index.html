Prince Gupta

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Prince Jitendrakumar Gupta(Born on 12 March 2003) is an Indian Web Developer and Web Designer and works at Indian Administrative Service(IAS) At Gujarat State. Gupta has made contributions to his engineering fields and welfare for people of nations. He works for people and welfare for people and well-being of people.

He is currently an IAS Officer of Amreli District at Gujarat State. He was awarded the best Administrative Service Medal for his good records in Year 2030. He secure AIR 144 In UPSC.
